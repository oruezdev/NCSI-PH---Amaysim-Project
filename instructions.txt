Please read the following instructions on how to navigate the site:

1) Download all the files and open index.html file

2) Add Product To Cart
    To choose a product:
        a) Browse catalouge and click "ADD TO CART" button

3) Increasing/Decreasing Product Quantity
    To increase product quantity:
        a) Click the "ADD TO CART" button to increase quantity
        b) Hover to the quantity of product and you  may click the arrow up key to increase quantity
        
    To decrease product quantity:
        a) Hover to the quantity of product and you  may click the arrow down key to decrease quantity

4) Product Removal 
    To remove a product from your cart: 
        a) Click "REMOVE" button

5) Discounts
    a) Unlimited 1GB
        3 for 2 deal: For the first month, you will get 3 Unlimited 1GB for the price of 2
        
        To activate this offer:
            - Add 3 Unlimited 1GB to cart 
            - If there are 3 Unlimited 1GB in the cart, discount will be automatically applied and show how much the discount is
            - A message will appear: "Enjoy the first 3 Unlimited 1GB Sim for the price of 2!"
    
    b) Unlimited 2GB
        Bundle deal: For every 2 Unlimited 2GB sold, there will be a free 1 GB Data-pack
        
        To activate this offer:
            - Add 2 or multiples of 2 Unlimited 2GB to cart 
            - If there are 2 Unlimited 2GB in the cart, a 1 GB Data-pack (bundle) will be automatically added to your cart
            - The slashed off price from the freebie will be shown on the "Discount" to show the user how much they have saved
            - A message will appear: "Free 1 GB Data-pack free-of-charge for every Unlimited 2GB"

    c) Unlimited 5GB
        Bulk deal: For bulk orders that is more than 3, the price will drop to $39.90 each

        To activate this offer:
            - Add at least 4 Unlimited 5GB to cart 
            - Discount will be automatically applied and show how much the discount is
            - A message will appear: "Bulk discount applied! Unlimited 5GB price dropped from $44.90 to $39.90 each for 3 or more items purchased"

6) Applying Promo Code
    a) To apply promo code: enter promo code "I<3AMAYSIM" on the text box provided
    b) Click "APPLY" button
        I) If the promo code is valid:
            - An alert will pop-up: "Promo code activated!"
            - A message will appear: "Enjoy additional 10% discount on your purchase."
            - 10% discount will be deducted to the Total price
        II) If the promo is invalid:
            - An alert will show pop-up: "Invalid promo code!"
            - Characters will be removed to reset text box input

7) Checking Out
    a) To checkout, click the "CHECKOUT" button
    b) After checking out, the cart will be empty
